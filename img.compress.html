How Our Image Compressor Works

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to reduce image file size while maintaining visual quality. Here's how to use it:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher values mean better quality but larger files)
  3. Select your desired output format (or keep the original format)
  4. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds
  5. Download your optimized image

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to image size?

Yes, the current limit is 10MB per image. For larger images, consider resizing them first.

Which format should I choose?

WebP generally offers the best compression. JPEG is good for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency.
